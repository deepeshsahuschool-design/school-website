Who We Are

A Tradition of Excellence

Founded in 1998, Little Stars Primary School has been a beacon of quality education for over two decades. We believe that every child is unique and has the potential to shine when provided with the right environment.

Our campus is designed to stimulate curiosity and foster a love for learning. From spacious classrooms to state-of-the-art labs and play areas, every corner of our school is built with the child in mind.
