Academics

Curriculum designed for the curious minds of Play School kids and classes 1 to 5.

Our Methodology

We follow a child-centric approach that emphasizes 'Learning by Doing'. Our curriculum is a blend of traditional values and modern teaching techniques, aligned with the latest educational standards.

Play Group

First steps into learning with a focus on sensory play, social interaction, and motor skill development.

Nursery

Fostering curiosity and early language skills through stories, creative activities, and guided exploration.

Junior KG

Introducing foundational academic concepts, pre-reading, pre-writing, and number recognition in a fun environment.

Senior KG

Preparing for formal schooling with enhanced literacy, numeracy, problem-solving, and independence.

Class 1

Focus on basic literacy, numeracy, and environmental awareness through play-way methods.

Class 2 & 3

Building on foundations with introduction to science, logical reasoning, and languages.

Class 4 & 5

Preparing for middle school with advanced concepts, project work, and critical thinking.
